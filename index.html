Proliferating passion in astronomy and astrophysics.

Origins
OAAO was founded by a group of students who saw the need for a high quality competition in Astronomy that anyone throughout the world could participate in. It is organized and run by Quest Andromeda, the parent organization for Astronomy enthusiasts.

Fully Online

Any student with access to technology can participate!

Individual and Team Competitions

Students can participate individually, or with a team of 3 students

High Quality and Unique Problems

Along with problems written by experienced competitors, portions of the competition focus on practical application and research - a unique opportunity to showcase your skills.

PRIZES!

For both the individual and team compeititons, there will be awards for the winners - money, observation equipment, and more!
